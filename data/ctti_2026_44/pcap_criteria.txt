ANNEX 2. CRITERIS D'ADJUDICACIÓ
Contract: CTTI-2026-44
Migració de la infraestructura d'equipaments als entorns de núvol híbrid

SCORING OVERVIEW
Total maximum score: 100 points
- Criteria evaluated by judgement of value (judici de valor): maximum 49 points
- Criteria evaluated automatically (fórmules): maximum 51 points

=============================================================
SECTION 2.a — CRITERIA EVALUATED BY JUDGEMENT OF VALUE (49 points)
=============================================================

For each criterion evaluable by judgement of value, the technical evaluation
will be carried out attending to the assessable aspects indicated in each criterion.

Where an offer equals or exceeds 90% of the technical valuation of a criterion
with its own identity (CIP), the final score will be obtained by applying the
formula from Directriu 1/2020:

Pop = P * (VTop / VTmv)

Where:
- Pop = score for the criterion for the offer being evaluated
- P = maximum score for the criterion
- VTop = technical valuation of the criterion for the offer being scored
- VTmv = technical valuation of the criterion with the best valuation

---

CRITERION 1.1 — PLA DE MIGRACIÓ DE SISTEMES (CIP)
Maximum points: 10

Reference sections in PPT: apartats 2.1 i 5.1

What to evaluate:
The proposed migration plan and assigned project team will be evaluated, attending
to the qualifications and certifications of the resources, the quantity, the
dedicated allocation across each project phase, and references from comparable
public-sector migrations of equivalent scale and complexity. The phasing approach
and dependency management between workstreams will also be assessed.

---

CRITERION 1.2 — ARQUITECTURA TÈCNICA PROPOSADA (CIP)
Maximum points: 20

Reference sections in PPT: apartats 2.2, 2.3, 5.2 i 5.3

What to evaluate:

- The design of the proposed hybrid cloud architecture, including the integration
  of Azure and/or AWS hyperscaler services with the private cloud environment at
  the Barcelona Supercomputing Center (BSC), will be evaluated. The coherence of
  the proposed topology and the justification for workload placement decisions
  will be assessed.
  (maximum 6.00 points)

- The resilience and high availability design, including failover mechanisms,
  geographic distribution of workloads, recovery time objectives (RTO) and
  recovery point objectives (RPO) per service tier, will be evaluated. The
  credibility of the proposed SLA commitments will be assessed.
  (maximum 7.00 points)

- Interoperability with existing CTTI systems and infrastructure, including API
  gateway design, identity federation with the existing CTTI LDAP/Active Directory
  environment, network segmentation and connectivity to the Generalitat's xarxa
  troncal, will be evaluated. The quality of the integration design will be assessed.
  (maximum 7.00 points)

---

CRITERION 1.3 — SEGURETAT I COMPLIMENT NORMATIU (CIP)
Maximum points: 12

Reference sections in PPT: apartats 2.4 i 5.4

What to evaluate:
The security architecture and regulatory compliance approach will be evaluated,
attending specifically to:
- Alignment with the Esquema Nacional de Seguretat (ENS) at the required
  classification level, including controls mapping and certification evidence.
- Compliance with GDPR requirements for personal data processing, with particular
  attention to data residency constraints and the sovereignty of data processed
  on behalf of Catalan public bodies.
- Data sovereignty architecture: measures to ensure that data subject to Catalan
  and Spanish regulatory frameworks does not transit or reside in jurisdictions
  that conflict with applicable obligations.
- Encryption in transit and at rest, identity and access management controls,
  and security monitoring integration with the Agència de Ciberseguretat de
  Catalunya's existing platforms.

---

CRITERION 1.4 — PLA DE TRANSICIÓ DEL SERVEI (CIP)
Maximum points: 7

Reference sections in PPT: apartat 5.5

What to evaluate:
The content and credibility of the proposed service handover plan will be
evaluated, including the dimensioning and qualification of the dedicated transition
team, the knowledge transfer programme for CTTI technical staff, the duration
and structure of the post-migration support period, and the mechanisms proposed
to ensure service continuity throughout the transition.
