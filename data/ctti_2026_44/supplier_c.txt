=====================================================================
EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
=====================================================================

InfraTech Catalunya SA is a technology solutions provider with extensive experience
in cloud infrastructure services for enterprise and public-sector clients. We have
supported organisations across Catalonia and the broader Iberian market through
their cloud adoption journeys and are well positioned to deliver the hybrid cloud
migration required under this tender. Our proposal reflects our broad cloud
capability and our commitment to delivering reliable, compliant infrastructure
for the Generalitat de Catalunya.

=====================================================================
PLA DE MIGRACIÓ DE SISTEMES — PROJECT TEAM AND MIGRATION PLAN
=====================================================================

Our project will be led by a senior project manager with significant experience
in ICT infrastructure projects. He brings strong organisational and client
management skills and has overseen a range of technology delivery engagements
across multiple sectors, including public administration. He holds PMP
certification and is supported by our established project governance framework.

The solution architect assigned to this engagement brings broad cloud architecture
experience across Azure and AWS environments. He has participated in cloud adoption
projects for enterprise clients in the retail and manufacturing sectors and holds
Azure Solutions Architect Expert certification. His experience in hybrid cloud
designs combining hyperscaler and private cloud environments is described in the
team CVs attached as Annex B, though his prior engagements in the public sector
are limited.

Our migration team is drawn from our cloud practice, which has completed in excess
of thirty cloud migration projects over the past four years. Two engineers are
assigned to this contract. Both are certified at the associate level on Azure.
The team has experience with lift-and-shift migration patterns and has used Azure
Migrate tooling on previous engagements.

The migration plan submitted with this proposal follows a wave-based approach
aligned with workload classification. The plan outlines four migration waves
and includes a timeline for each. Rollback procedures are described at a general
level, committing to the principle that rollback from any wave will be possible
within the cutover window; specific rollback runbooks will be developed during
Phase 1. Risk management will follow our standard project risk framework,
adapted for the specific characteristics of the CTTI environment following the
Phase 1 workload inventory.

=====================================================================
ARQUITECTURA TÈCNICA PROPOSADA
=====================================================================

The proposed architecture is based on Microsoft Azure as the primary hyperscaler,
with BSC private cloud hosting for the workload categories requiring physical
data residency within Catalonia. Connectivity is provided via ExpressRoute with
private peering to BSC, supplemented by site-to-site VPN as a secondary path.

For CRITICAL tier services, we propose an active-passive configuration with
automated failover. Our proposed RTO for CRITICAL services is 4 hours, meeting
the PPT minimum. RPO is proposed at 1 hour. The failover mechanism uses Azure
Traffic Manager with health check polling at 10-second intervals.

Identity and access management uses Azure AD Connect for federation with the
existing CTTI Active Directory environment. Our proposal notes that the detailed
migration approach for group policy objects and Conditional Access policies will
be defined during the Phase 1 assessment, as the specific configuration of the
CTTI Active Directory environment is not yet fully known to us.

Monitoring integration will be implemented to export telemetry data to CTTI's
collection platform. The specific protocols and telemetry configuration will
be aligned with CTTI requirements during the Phase 1 integration design workshop.

We note that the BSC private cloud integration design detailed in this proposal
is based on publicly available information about BSC infrastructure capabilities.
A more precise integration architecture will be developed once BSC's current
connectivity specifications and available APIs are confirmed during Phase 1.

=====================================================================
SEGURETAT I COMPLIMENT NORMATIU
=====================================================================

InfraTech Catalunya is in the process of pursuing ENS MEDI certification for its
cloud services. Certification is expected to be completed within 9 months of
contract award. In the interim period, InfraTech will implement ENS MEDI controls
as a managed obligation and will provide CTTI with a self-assessment controls
mapping within 45 days of contract start. An independent audit to support
formal certification will be commissioned within 3 months of contract award.

GDPR compliance measures include encryption of personal data at rest and in
transit, with encryption keys managed within Azure Key Vault. All data in scope
will be stored within the EEA. A data flow map covering the in-scope processing
operations will be provided within 90 days of contract start.

Security monitoring integration with the Agència de Ciberseguretat de Catalunya's
platforms is included in the proposal scope. The specific integration format and
delivery mechanism will be confirmed during the Phase 1 security design workshop.

Our security approach follows industry best practices and applicable regulatory
frameworks, and our team is experienced in deploying secure cloud infrastructure
for regulated sectors. We are committed to meeting all security requirements set
out in the PPT throughout the contract period.

=====================================================================
PLA DE TRANSICIÓ DEL SERVEI
=====================================================================

InfraTech Catalunya's handover plan provides for two structured knowledge
transfer sessions of 8 hours each, meeting the PPT minimum requirement.
Sessions will cover architecture overview and operational procedures.
All documentation will be provided in Catalan.

A 6-month post-handover warranty support period is included. The transition
team will be drawn from the project team members who have been involved in
the migration, providing continuity of knowledge for the handover process.
Detailed handover documentation will be prepared and delivered prior to the
start of the handover period. The format and content of the knowledge base
will be aligned with CTTI's documentation standards during the project.
