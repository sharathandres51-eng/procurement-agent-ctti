=====================================================================
EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
=====================================================================

GuardNet Partners SL presents this proposal for CTTI tender CTTI-2026-51.
GuardNet is a managed security services provider with eight years of experience
in the Spanish market, focused on public-sector and regulated-industry clients.
Our strength is the structure, transparency and accountability of our governance
and reporting model, which has been recognised in two consecutive years by our
public-sector clients as the most consistent and reliable in their vendor portfolio.
We are candid in this proposal about areas where we are growing our capability.

=====================================================================
EQUIP I QUALIFICACIONS DEL SOC
=====================================================================

The contract will be managed by Ana Torres as SOC Manager, holding CISM
certification and 6 years of information security experience, of which 3 are
in managed security service delivery roles for public administration clients.
She is dedicated 100% to this contract as its primary service manager.

Two L2 analysts are assigned to this contract, both with 3 years of SOC
experience. One holds GIAC GCIH certification; the second holds CompTIA CySA+
and has been enrolled in GCIH training expected to complete within 8 months
of contract start. Their experience is primarily with Spanish public-sector
monitoring environments, which we consider an advantage for CTTI onboarding
speed and contextual threat awareness.

Four L1 analysts staff the 24/7 shift rotation, all holding CompTIA Security+
and with a minimum of 1 year of SOC experience. Shift staffing is structured
to ensure at least one analyst on duty at all hours, with call-in procedures
for the overnight window. We note that this is our minimum coverage commitment;
additional L1 headcount can be activated within 48 hours if event volumes
increase significantly during the contract term.

L3/IR capability is provided by GuardNet's IR practice, which is a shared
resource across the GuardNet account portfolio. Our IR lead holds GIAC GCFE
certification and has 4 years of IR experience, including two major incident
engagements for Spanish public administration clients. He is not dedicated
exclusively to this contract. GuardNet has an ongoing relationship with the
Agència de Ciberseguretat de Catalunya from a prior advisory engagement.

CTI capability is provided by a shared CTI analyst with 2 years of threat
intelligence experience. This is an area where GuardNet is actively investing;
a second CTI analyst is scheduled to join the team within 6 months.

=====================================================================
CAPACITAT DE DETECCIÓ I RESPOSTA
=====================================================================

GuardNet's SIEM platform is IBM QRadar SIEM, deployed on dedicated on-premise
hardware in a Tier III data centre in Spain, with log replication to a secondary
site for resilience. Current ingestion capacity is 40,000 EPS, with a documented
scale path to 100,000 EPS via hardware expansion. We acknowledge that the 50,000
EPS baseline and 150,000 EPS scale target of the PPT represents a stretch target;
our proposal commits to the 50,000 EPS baseline and to a phased capacity
expansion plan reaching 120,000 EPS within 12 months of contract start.

Log retention is configured for 12 months online and 36 months in cold archive,
meeting the PPT requirement.

The detection use case library at contract start comprises 165 documented use
cases mapped to MITRE ATT&CK, covering 14 of the 15 ATT&CK tactics identified
as relevant to Spanish public administration in CCN-CERT advisories. A gap
analysis against the missing tactic has identified 8 new use cases to be
developed and deployed within 90 days of contract start.

SOAR automation is provided via IBM QRadar SOAR. Automated triage covers 60%
of P3/P4 alerts. Playbooks are documented in PDF format; migration to a machine-
readable YAML format is on the GuardNet product roadmap for H2 2026.

For P1 incidents, the L3 IR lead is paged within 10 minutes of P1 classification.
IR coordination procedures with the Agència de Ciberseguretat have been
discussed at a working level and a formal MOU is in progress; the coordination
workflow is documented in our IR runbook but is not yet formally agreed with
the Agència.

=====================================================================
INTEL·LIGÈNCIA D'AMENACES
=====================================================================

GuardNet operationalises CCN-CERT REYES (mandatory), AlienVault OTX and MISP
community feeds. A commercial feed from Recorded Future is under active
procurement and is expected to be operational within 3 months of contract start.
Critical IoCs are operationalised within 4 hours of receipt, meeting the PPT target.

Monthly threat intelligence reports are delivered by the shared CTI analyst.
Threat hunting is conducted monthly with reports delivered within 5 business
days, meeting the PPT boundary. Prior contributions to CCN-CERT or national
ISAC communities are not referenced in this proposal, as GuardNet's CTI function
is in an early stage of maturity in this regard.

=====================================================================
REPORTING I GOVERNANÇA
=====================================================================

GuardNet's governance model is the most comprehensively documented aspect of
this proposal. Operational reporting is delivered weekly (dashboard summary)
and monthly (full operational report with SLA evidence, incident analysis,
trend data and recommendations). Executive reporting is delivered quarterly,
with a tailored format co-designed with each client during onboarding.

SLA definitions are defined per PPT Section 3.1.3 with measurement methodology
documented and agreed in a Service Level Schedule (SLS) signed at contract start.
All SLA measurements are independently verifiable from QRadar log data available
to CTTI.

Escalation for P1 incidents follows a four-tier path with defined notification
timelines at each step, up to and including GuardNet CEO notification for
incidents affecting critical Generalitat services. This escalation framework
is fully documented in Annex C and was audited by an independent consultant
in Q4 2025.

Monthly service review meetings are conducted with a defined agenda and
action-tracking process. Quarterly executive reviews include a forward-looking
risk and threat landscape session. An annual service maturity assessment against
a defined capability maturity model is provided to CTTI at no additional cost.
