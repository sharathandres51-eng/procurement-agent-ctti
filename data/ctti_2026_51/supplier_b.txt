Serveis gestionats de Centre d'Operacions de Seguretat (SOC) per a la Generalitat de Catalunya

=====================================================================
EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
=====================================================================

SecOps Iberia SA is pleased to submit this proposal for CTTI tender CTTI-2026-51.
SecOps Iberia operates one of the largest managed SOC platforms in the Iberian
market, built on the Splunk Enterprise Security SIEM and Splunk SOAR platform.
Our technology stack is best-in-class and our detection and automation capabilities
are at the leading edge of the industry. We believe our platform is the strongest
in this procurement and we have structured this proposal to demonstrate that.

=====================================================================
EQUIP I QUALIFICACIONS DEL SOC
=====================================================================

The contract SOC Manager will be David Herrero, holding CISSP certification and
6 years of information security experience, with 2 years in a SOC management role.
He is assigned at 40% dedication to this contract, with the remaining 60% split
across two other managed SOC accounts. His day-to-day operational availability
to CTTI is supplemented by a deputy SOC Manager who provides coverage during
David's non-dedicated hours.

Two L2 analysts are assigned to this contract, both holding CEH certification
and 3 years of SOC experience. Neither holds GIAC-family certifications at this
time, though SecOps Iberia has enrolled both in GCIH training programmes expected
to conclude within 12 months of contract start.

Four L1 analysts staff the 24/7 shift pattern. All hold CompTIA Security+ and
have a minimum of 1 year of SOC experience. One L1 analyst is shared with another
SecOps managed SOC account during the 02:00–06:00 overnight window, with call-in
procedures documented for surge scenarios during that period.

L3 and IR specialist capability is provided by SecOps Iberia's central IR team,
which is shared across all managed accounts. The IR team holds GIAC GCFE and
OSCP certifications and has significant IR experience, including involvement in
response to three ransomware incidents at Spanish infrastructure operators in
2023–2024. A named primary IR contact is assigned to this contract, but he is
not dedicated exclusively to it.

The CTI function is also provided from a shared central team. A CTI analyst
is assigned to this account for the delivery of monthly reports and threat
hunting engagements; this analyst is shared across four other SecOps accounts.

=====================================================================
CAPACITAT DE DETECCIÓ I RESPOSTA
=====================================================================

SecOps Iberia's platform is built on Splunk Enterprise Security with Splunk SOAR
for automation and orchestration. Splunk ES is deployed on dedicated cloud
infrastructure for this contract, with CTTI granted read-only access to a
dedicated Splunk instance. The platform is currently ingesting at 85,000 EPS
across the managed account estate; the CTTI contract will be deployed on a
dedicated cluster capacity of up to 200,000 EPS, the highest scale commitment
in this procurement.

Log retention is implemented as 12 months in Splunk SmartStore (searchable) and
36 months in Amazon S3 Glacier (cold), meeting the PPT requirement.

Our detection use case library comprises 312 documented use cases at contract
start, mapped to MITRE ATT&CK. This is the largest library in this procurement.
Coverage spans 20 ATT&CK tactics. New use cases are deployed within 4 business
days of a CCN-CERT alta or higher advisory.

SOAR playbooks are fully documented in YAML format and stored in a version-
controlled repository. CTTI will have read access to the playbook repository and
a defined change approval process for playbooks affecting production CTTI systems.
Automated triage handles 78% of P3/P4 alerts without analyst intervention,
freeing capacity for higher-severity events.

The IR workflow for P1 incidents includes automatic paging of the assigned
IR contact within 8 minutes of P1 classification. Coordination procedures with
the Agència de Ciberseguretat de Catalunya are documented but have not yet
been formally tested with the agency in a tabletop exercise format.

=====================================================================
INTEL·LIGÈNCIA D'AMENACES
=====================================================================

SecOps Iberia operationalises Mandiant Advantage (commercial), CCN-CERT REYES
(mandatory) and AlienVault OTX (open source) feeds. The proposal notes that a
second open-source feed is under evaluation for addition to the stack; it is not
yet confirmed. Critical IoCs are operationalised within 3 hours of receipt.

Monthly threat intelligence reports are delivered from the shared CTI team.
Threat hunting is conducted monthly. Hunting reports are delivered within
5 business days of each engagement, at the PPT boundary. The CTI analyst
assigned to this account has 2 years of threat intelligence experience.
Prior contributions to CCN-CERT or public-sector ISAC communities are not
referenced in this proposal.

=====================================================================
REPORTING I GOVERNANÇA
=====================================================================

Operational reporting is delivered monthly via a Splunk dashboard report in
PDF and Excel formats. Executive reporting is delivered quarterly. SLA metrics
are tracked automatically within Splunk and are available via the CTTI read-only
instance at any time.

The governance model is described in the proposal as following SecOps Iberia's
standard managed service governance framework, with monthly operational reviews
and quarterly strategic reviews. The escalation procedure for P1 incidents is
documented in the service operations manual (provided as Annex D), though the
specific escalation timelines and the names of individuals in the escalation
path above SOC Manager level are not included in this Sobre B submission.

Service reviews are conducted by the SOC Manager. Senior SecOps Iberia
management are available for escalation reviews when required; the cadence
and trigger conditions for senior management involvement are not specified.
