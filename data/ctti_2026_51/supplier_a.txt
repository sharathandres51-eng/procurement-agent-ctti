SOBRE B — TECHNICAL PROPOSAL
Company: CyberShield Catalunya SL
Tender: CTTI-2026-51
Serveis gestionats de Centre d'Operacions de Seguretat (SOC) per a la Generalitat de Catalunya

=====================================================================
EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
=====================================================================

CyberShield Catalunya SL presents this technical proposal for CTTI tender
CTTI-2026-51. Our managed SOC service is purpose-built for public-sector clients
and has been providing 24/7 security monitoring to two other Catalan public
administrations since 2022. Our team's certifications and our threat intelligence
capability are the strongest aspects of this proposal, and we believe they
represent a meaningful differentiator in a market where analyst quality varies
significantly.

=====================================================================

=====================================================================

Marc holds CISSP and CISM certifications and has 9 years of information security
experience, of which 5 have been spent leading SOC operations for public-sector

Two L2 analysts are assigned full-time to this contract, both holding GIAC GCIH

all holding CompTIA Security+ and with a minimum of 2 years of L1 SOC experience.

=====================================================================
CAPACITAT DE DETECCIÓ I RESPOSTA
=====================================================================

CyberShield operates a Microsoft Sentinel SIEM with a Palo Alto XSOAR SOAR
platform for automation and orchestration. Sentinel is deployed in the West Europe
Azure region with a dedicated workspace for this contract, providing CTTI with
read-only access via a CTTI-managed Entra ID principal.

The platform currently ingests at 65,000 EPS and is architected to scale to
180,000 EPS via workspace scaling without architectural change. Log retention
is configured for 12 months hot (Sentinel Analytics) and 36 months cold
(Azure Data Lake tier), meeting the PPT requirement.

Our detection use case library at contract start comprises 247 documented use
cases, all mapped to MITRE ATT&CK. Coverage spans 18 ATT&CK tactics, including
all 15 tactics identified as relevant to Spanish public administration in
CCN-CERT advisories from 2023–2025. New use cases are deployed within 3 business
days of a CCN-CERT alta or higher advisory, inside the 5-day PPT target.

Incident response workflow follows a documented 5-stage process: Detect > Triage
> Contain > Eradicate > Recover. For P1 incidents, the L3 analyst is paged
automatically within 5 minutes of P1 classification. Coordination with the
Agència de Ciberseguretat de Catalunya uses a pre-agreed notification template
and a dedicated secure channel, tested annually during IR simulation exercises.

=====================================================================
INTEL·LIGÈNCIA D'AMENACES
=====================================================================

CyberShield's threat intelligence programme for this contract operationalises
feeds from Recorded Future (commercial), CCN-CERT REYES (mandatory), MISP
community (open source) and AlienVault OTX (open source). Critical IoCs are
operationalised in Sentinel within 2 hours of receipt, inside the 4-hour PPT target.

The dedicated CTI analyst delivers a monthly threat landscape report tailored
to the Catalan public administration threat environment, drawing on intelligence
from CCN-CERT, ENISA and the Spanish public sector ISAC. Threat hunting is
conducted monthly using a documented hypothesis-driven methodology aligned with
the MITRE ATT&CK framework. Hunting reports are delivered within 4 business
days of each engagement.

In 2024, our CTI team identified and shared with CCN-CERT two previously
undocumented IoC clusters associated with a phishing campaign targeting Spanish
local government entities. This contribution resulted in a CCN-CERT advisory
referencing our intelligence. References available on request.

=====================================================================
REPORTING I GOVERNANÇA
=====================================================================

Operational reports are delivered monthly, covering: alert volumes by severity,
MTTD and MTTR per tier, SLA compliance, incident summary and open recommendations.
Executive reports are delivered quarterly, covering: threat landscape summary,
service performance trend, upcoming risk factors and strategic recommendations.

SLA measurement is automated via Sentinel workbook dashboards, with data
available to CTTI in real time. Service review meetings are held monthly with
the SOC Manager and CTTI's designated service owner. An annual contract review
meeting is held with senior stakeholders from both organisations.

Escalation for P1 incidents follows a three-tier path: L3 analyst > SOC Manager
(15-minute response obligation) > CyberShield CISO (30-minute response obligation
for incidents affecting critical Generalitat services).
