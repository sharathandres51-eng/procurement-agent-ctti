=====================================================================
EQUIPMENT TECHNICAL OFFER
=====================================================================

The equipment proposed by SecureComms Catalunya is fully compliant with all
applicable ETSI standards, including ETSI GS QKD 004 and 014, and exceeds the
minimum performance requirements specified in Section 3.1.2 of the PPT. The
proposed system delivers superior key generation rates under the expected operating
conditions of the CTTI topology.

The KMS component incorporates industry-leading security features and meets all
relevant compliance requirements for cryptographic modules. Our equipment has been
validated through appropriate certification processes and is widely deployed in
comparable environments. All units are supplied in rack format compatible with
standard telecommunications cabinets. Redundancy features are incorporated to
ensure high availability of the quantum key distribution service.

Emitter and receiver units are independently replaceable, in line with the
flexibility requirements of the tender. The proposed equipment is designed to
accommodate changes in network topology without requiring full system redesign.

=====================================================================
DEPLOYMENT RISK MANAGEMENT
=====================================================================

SecureComms Catalunya has conducted a risk assessment of the deployment scope
and has identified the following risks as the most relevant to this project.

Risk 1 — Network connectivity disruption during installation: The installation
activities carry a risk of temporary service impact on existing network
infrastructure at the deployment sites. Probability: medium. Impact: temporary
disruption to operational services at affected nodes. Mitigation: standard change
management procedures will be applied, with installation windows agreed with CTTI
operations teams in advance of each site visit.

Risk 2 — Equipment delivery delays: Supply chain conditions may affect the
availability of some equipment components within the originally planned delivery
schedule. Probability: low to medium. Impact: potential delay to the commissioning
milestone. Mitigation: SecureComms Catalunya has contingency stock arrangements
with our suppliers to mitigate the risk of delivery delays affecting the project
timeline.

Our risk management framework will be applied throughout the project to identify
and address emerging risks as the deployment progresses.
