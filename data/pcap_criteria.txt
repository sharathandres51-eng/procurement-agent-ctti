ANNEX 2. CRITERIS D'ADJUDICACIÓ
Contract: CTTI-2026-36
Subministrament, instal·lació i posada en producció d'equipaments de distribució de clau quàntica

SCORING OVERVIEW
Total maximum score: 100 points
- Criteria evaluated by judgement of value (judici de valor): maximum 49 points
- Criteria evaluated automatically (fórmules): maximum 51 points

=============================================================
SECTION 2.a — CRITERIA EVALUATED BY JUDGEMENT OF VALUE (49 points)
=============================================================

For each criterion evaluable by judgement of value, the technical evaluation
will be carried out attending to the assessable aspects indicated in each criterion.

Where an offer equals or exceeds 90% of the technical valuation of a criterion
with its own identity (CIP), the final score will be obtained by applying the
formula from Directriu 1/2020:

Pop = P * (VTop / VTmv)

Where:
- Pop = score for the criterion for the offer being evaluated
- P = maximum score for the criterion
- VTop = technical valuation of the criterion for the offer being scored
- VTmv = technical valuation of the criterion with the best valuation

CRITERION 1.1 — PLA DE MIGRACIÓ (CIP)
Maximum points: 9

Reference sections in PPT: apartats 2.1 i 5.1

What to evaluate:
The proposed service team for the migration plan will be evaluated, attending to
the qualifications of the resources, the quantity, the dedication and the references
provided, in order to guarantee the quality fulfilment of the milestones and
timelines of the phase.

---

CRITERION 1.2 — EXECUCIÓ CRÍTICA I DESPLEGAMENT (CIP)
Maximum points: 30

Reference sections in PPT: apartats 2.2, 2.3, 5.2 i 5.3

What to evaluate:

- The proposed service team for Critical Execution and Deployment will be evaluated,
  attending to the qualifications of the resources, the quantity, the dedication and
  the references provided, in order to guarantee the quality fulfilment of the
  milestones and timelines of the project phases.
  (maximum 4.50 points)

- The identification of up to a maximum of 3 risks during node deployment will be
  evaluated, taking into account that they have a high probability and impact on the
  service, and the mitigation proposals for these, considering the level of impact of
  the risk, the speed and ease of executing the mitigation proposal and the
  identification of all the conditions to guarantee the migration capacity during the
  contract. Each correctly identified risk with a specific mitigation proposal will
  be evaluated with a maximum of 1.00 point per risk.
  (maximum 3.00 points)

- Service monitoring tool. Having the service monitoring tool at the start of the
  service in a fully operational state and with all its capabilities will be evaluated.
  It will also be taken into account that it is accessible, simple and updated with
  a frequency higher than the required minimums, as well as providing a global and
  granular view of the entire execution: KPIs provided, degree of compliance,
  validation and certification, including risks, with the objective of having control
  of the state of the service.
  (maximum 1.50 points)

- Documentation. It will be evaluated that the proposed documentation and reports
  ensure correct project execution and allow, if necessary, the commissioning of the
  service by a third party.
  (maximum 1.50 points)

- Supply of equipment. The following will be evaluated:
  o The characteristics of the equipment that increase flexibility and adaptation to
    changes in the network architecture, such as when a connectivity passes from DWDM
    to dark fibre, or when connecting two locations it is necessary to add or remove
    hops.
    (maximum 5.50 points)
  o The functionalities and technical specifications that increase the service level
    and the capabilities of the proposed equipment, such as security functionalities,
    performance optimisation, interface and monitoring.
    (maximum 8.50 points)
  o The parameters and/or components that improve the robustness and resilience
    against the probability of equipment failure. The quantity and criticality of
    redundant components will be taken into account.
    (maximum 5.50 points)
